Two Dham Yatra(Choose any Two)

Embark on the Divine Path: Book Your Cab for the Char Dham Yatra

YAMUNOTRI (यमुनोत्री)

Begin your spiritual journey with Yamunotri, the sacred source of the Yamuna River, nestled in the pristine Himalayas.

  • Visit the revered Yamunotri Temple dedicated to Goddess Yamuna.
  • Witness the thermal springs of Surya Kund.
  • Enjoy the scenic trek through lush meadows and dense forests.
  • Experience the serene beauty of the Bandarpunch Peak.

Let Shanaya Tours & Travels guide you to Yamunotri, ensuring a peaceful and divine start to your Char Dham Yatra. Embrace the tranquility and spiritual essence of this holy destination with us.

GANGOTRI (गंगोत्री)

Immerse yourself in the spiritual purity of Gangotri, the sacred origin of the Ganges River, and a vital part of the Char Dham pilgrimage.

  • Pay homage at the ancient Gangotri Temple.
  • Witness the stunning beauty of the Bhagirathi River.
  • Explore the sacred rock where King Bhagirath meditated.
  • Experience the mystical charm of the Gaumukh Glacier.

With Shanaya Tours & Travels, your journey to Gangotri will be comfortable and spiritually uplifting. Let us take you to the heart of devotion and natural beauty.

KEDARNATH (केदारनाथ)

Discover the spiritual majesty of Kedarnath, home to one of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, set against the dramatic backdrop of the Himalayas.

  • Visit the ancient and revered Kedarnath Temple.
  • Marvel at the breathtaking views of the snow-clad peaks.
  • Experience the serenity of the Mandakini River.
  • Trek through scenic landscapes and spiritual paths.

Join Shanaya Tours & Travels for a profound pilgrimage to Kedarnath, where divinity and natural splendor converge. Let us make your spiritual journey memorable and serene.

Badrinath (बद्रीनाथ)

Complete your Char Dham Yatra with a visit to Badrinath, the sacred abode of Lord Vishnu, nestled between the Nar and Narayan mountain ranges.

  • Pay respects at the historic Badrinath Temple.
  • Witness the confluence of the Alaknanda and Rishi Ganga rivers.
  • Explore the mystical Tapt Kund hot springs.
  • Experience the beauty of the Neelkanth Peak.

Shanaya Tours & Travels invites you to experience the divine grace and stunning beauty of Badrinath. Let us ensure a seamless and spiritually enriching journey to this revered destination.
